About

Meridian Tech LLC is a software company founded in 2025. We build custom software and AI-powered tools that help small businesses work smarter, serve customers better, and grow faster.

Our team brings 7+ years of experience in high-volume operations and logistics at Fortune 500 companies, combined with hands-on software engineering expertise. We understand both the technical and operational sides of building software that actually works for real businesses.

Our Mission

To make powerful technology accessible to small businesses. We believe every business deserves software that works as hard as they do — not just the ones with big budgets. We're here to close that gap by building affordable, AI-driven tools that solve real problems and create real value.

Our Vision

A world where small businesses have the same technological advantages as large enterprises. We envision a future where AI and custom software aren't luxuries — they're standard tools that help every business owner spend less time on operations and more time doing what they love.

What Drives Us

At the end of the day, we're builders who want to help people. We started Meridian Tech because we saw too many small businesses struggling with outdated tools, manual processes, and software that wasn't built for them. We believe technology should make your life easier, not harder. Every project we take on starts with one question: how can we genuinely help this business succeed?
